Department List

Follow the steps below to add a new branch:

First, successfully login to the software.

First select the payroll from the administration. A new list will then appear.
Step-1
Select the Department section from the Human Resources section of the list.

Step-2
Add the department and click the Add button.

As soon as the department is added, the name of the department will be displayed on the screen.

Step-3
If you want to close the department, click the Close button.

Step-4
If you want to change the name of the department, click the Edit button.

A form will appear after clicking the Edit button.

Step-5
Change the name of the department and click the Update button.

Step-6
Click the Close button to close the form.
