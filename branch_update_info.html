Step 1 After selecting the section, a new list will appear. Select the Branch section from the Human Resources section of the list. A new list will appear after selecting the section. Step 2 Select the Updated Branch Information section from the list. After clicking on section T, a list of all the branches will be displayed. Step 3 If you want to close the branch, click the close button. Step 4 Click the Edit button to update the branch's new information. A new form will appear after clicking the Update button. Fill out the form correctly. Step 5 When done, click the Update button. Step 6 If you want to close the form, click the close button.
