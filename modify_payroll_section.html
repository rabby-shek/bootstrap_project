Modify Payroll Section

Follow these steps to keep track of your salary: -

First, successfully login to the software.

First select the payroll from the administration.
A new list will then appear.

Step-1
Select the payroll section of the control panel from the list that appears.

A few more lists will appear after clicking on the section.

Step-2
Select the modify payroll options section from the list.

Step-3
After clicking on the section, a list of all active employees will be displayed.

Click the Modify button for specific employee payroll options.

After clicking the modify button, a new page will open.

Step-4
Click the Delete button to modify.

After clicking the Delete button, permission will be ask to modify the data.

Step-5
Click the yes button to modify the data.

Step-6
If you do not want to modify the data, click the Cancel button.
