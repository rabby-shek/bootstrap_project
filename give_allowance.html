Give Allowance

Follow the steps below to pay the allowance:
First, successfully login to the software.

Step-1
First click on the Salary section from the Administration option.

After clicking on the pay section, a list of new ones will appear.

Step-2
Select the Salary section from the list.

After clicking on the pay section another list will come up.

Step-3
Click on the allowance option from the list.

A form will appear after clicking. Fill out the form correctly.

Step-4
Then click the Save button.

Step-5
Click the Cancel button to delete the data.
