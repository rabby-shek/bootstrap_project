Deposite Update

Follow the steps below to update the deposit account information:-

Step-1
Let's click on the Deposit section from Accounting.

Step-2
After clicking on the Deposit section, a new list will appear.
From there, click on the Add Deposit Info section.

Step-3
Click the Edit button to update a specific member's information.

After clicking the Edit button, a form will appear. Fill in all the required information in the form correctly.

Step-4
Click the Update button.
